# Seismic Golem - First 12 Upgrades

## Upgrade Pool Goal
This first pool is designed for the MVP vertical slice only. It supports two clean build routes:
- Magma
- Pure Energy

The player should be able to mix paths, but each path should also stand alone as a coherent fantasy.

## Body Mutations
### 1. Magma Fists
Type: Body Mutation

Effect:
- light combo attacks apply Burn
- combo finisher leaves a short-lived heat burst

Purpose:
- first clear Magma identity pick

### 2. Faulted Knuckles
Type: Body Mutation

Effect:
- heavy attack gains bonus stagger damage
- if heavy hits only one target, deal bonus damage

Purpose:
- stronger duelist/bruiser feel

### 3. Basalt Frame
Type: Body Mutation

Effect:
- gain flat damage reduction
- move slightly slower

Purpose:
- tank option for safer runs

### 4. Overheated Stride
Type: Body Mutation

Effect:
- dash leaves a short lava trail
- enemies touching the trail gain Burn

Purpose:
- mobility turned into area control

### 5. Conductive Veins
Type: Body Mutation

Effect:
- melee hits on Shocked enemies deal bonus damage
- small visual energy arcs appear in body cracks

Purpose:
- bridge between body combat and Energy path

### 6. Resonant Plates
Type: Body Mutation

Effect:
- every third melee hit releases a tiny radial pulse
- low base damage, strong proc utility

Purpose:
- rhythm reward and hybrid synergy

## Core Mutations
### 7. Ember Core
Type: Core Mutation

Effect:
- Core Pulse applies Burn
- enemies that die while Burning explode for minor area damage

Purpose:
- reinforces Magma route through the chest Core

### 8. Seismic Vent
Type: Core Mutation

Effect:
- heavy attack causes a secondary delayed shockwave
- delayed wave deals area damage around impact point

Purpose:
- makes committed attacks more rewarding

### 9. Prism Overload
Type: Core Mutation

Effect:
- Overload State fires a small energy burst at the end of each combo finisher

Purpose:
- payoff pick for aggressive Energy runs

### 10. Arc Core
Type: Core Mutation

Effect:
- Core Pulse applies Shock
- Shocked enemies can chain damage to one nearby enemy

Purpose:
- clearest Energy identity pick

## Relics
### 11. Quarry Ring
Type: Relic

Effect:
- increase heavy attack area slightly
- heavy attack cooldown recovery improves after a kill

Purpose:
- simple, broad-value melee relic

### 12. Facet Echo
Type: Relic

Effect:
- using Core Pulse grants a brief buff to the next melee hit
- boosted hit deals bonus damage and extra impact force

Purpose:
- teaches active weaving between melee and Core play

## Design Notes
- At least one Magma upgrade and one Energy upgrade should appear early in each run.
- Body and Core picks should visibly change the hero through glow, cracks, trails, and particles.
- Even hybrid runs must still read as the same Seismic Golem with the same chest Core.

## Recommended Early Reward Curve
Room 1-2:
- introduce one simple Body mutation

Room 3-4:
- introduce one Core mutation

Room 5+:
- allow relics and stronger synergy hooks
