# Seismic Golem

## Art Prompts

Bu dosya concept art, mood image veya keyframe üretimi için prompt setidir.

## Global Prompt Base

```text
Create premium concept art for "Seismic Golem", a short stylized 3D adventure game.

The world is an ancient sanctum built from massive warm-brown stone architecture, deep shadow, sparse sacred geometry, and a single pink crystal energy language.

The visual goal is not generic fantasy and not sci-fi neon. It should feel quiet, monumental, sacred, minimal, and emotionally heavy.

Core rules:
- huge stone masses
- clean composition
- limited color palette
- pink crystal is the only strong accent
- environment exists to elevate the golem character
- no clutter
- no random props
- no overdesigned VFX
- no castle fantasy clichés
- no cyberpunk city aesthetics

Mood:
- ancient system
- sacred infrastructure
- silent rails
- hidden flow
- premium indie art game
```

## Character + Environment Keyframe

```text
Create a cinematic keyframe for "Seismic Golem".

Scene:
A solitary stone golem with a pink crystal embedded in its chest stands inside a vast ancient sanctum. The chamber is built from warm brown stone, cream worn surfaces, and deep shadow. The pink crystal gives off a subtle sacred glow.

Composition:
- golem in foreground or midground
- massive architecture around it
- huge sense of silence and scale
- minimal clutter
- strong silhouette

Style:
- stylized 3D concept art
- premium indie art direction
- restrained palette
- sacred, atmospheric, monumental
```

## Uyanış Odası Prompt

```text
Concept art for the opening chamber of Seismic Golem.

A huge, dark stone sanctum room with very little visual clutter. A stone golem has just awakened near the center-back of the chamber. The room is mostly empty, with faint broken rail lines in the floor and subtle dust in the air. A pink crystal in the golem's chest emits the first pulse of light.

Make the space feel ancient, sacred, and silent.
The room should feel much larger than the character.
No bright fantasy lighting. No decorative noise.
```

## İlk Koridor Prompt

```text
Concept art for the first corridor in Seismic Golem.

A monumental stone corridor with repeating heavy pillars on both sides, a long forward perspective, faint inactive rail lines embedded in the floor, and one distant altar node at the far end. The architecture should feel like sacred infrastructure, not a dungeon hallway.

Mood:
- heavy silence
- anticipation
- scale

Keep the composition clean and directional.
```

## Ray Düğümü Prompt

```text
Concept art for a rail activation chamber in Seismic Golem.

A small but meaningful sanctum node space with one altar-like stone device at the center and a broken stone barrier nearby. The environment is mostly stone and shadow, but the altar begins to respond to the golem's chest crystal with a soft pink resonance line in the floor.

This should feel like the first moment the world recognizes the player.
Not a combat room.
Not a tech lab.
```

## Combat Cebi Prompt

```text
Concept art for the first combat pocket in Seismic Golem.

A compact stone arena pocket inside the sanctum. One broken pillar fragment or large stone block gives shape to the space. A small guardian creature waits in shadow. The golem should still feel dominant and heavy, not fragile.

The scene should emphasize:
- strong silhouettes
- readable combat space
- minimal visual clutter
- tension without chaos
```

## Mühür Bahçesi Prompt

```text
Concept art for the Seal Garden in Seismic Golem.

A symmetrical ritual chamber made of warm stone, with two small altar nodes and one sealed central gate. The ground includes a writing zone or sacred inscription surface. The room should feel orderly, ceremonial, and rule-based.

This area represents recognition, authorization, and sacred system logic.
Keep it elegant, still, and monumental.
```

## Rezonans Geçidi Prompt

```text
Concept art for the Resonance Passage in Seismic Golem.

A rising stone passage leading upward toward a brighter sacred destination. The path is narrow and heavy, with a dark abyss visible below or to the sides. Far ahead, pink crystal reflections hint that something larger is waiting.

The scene should feel like an approach to a threshold.
No excessive platformer look.
No clutter.
```

## Vista Platformu Prompt

```text
Create a grand vista keyframe for Seismic Golem.

The stone golem stands on a large, mostly empty platform overlooking a vast sanctum. In the distance are larger altar masses, silent rail structures, and monumental stone architecture disappearing into shadow. A few restrained pink crystal light traces suggest the system is beginning to wake.

Composition goals:
- character small but readable in foreground
- sanctum massive in background
- very strong sense of scale
- premium minimalism
- awe without visual noise
```

## Sanctum Node Prompt

```text
Create concept art for a sanctum node in Seismic Golem that symbolizes one Seismic ecosystem structure without feeling like an advertisement.

It should look like:
- a sacred stone node
- an altar ring
- an inscription monument
- or a rail-linked sanctum plate

It must feel like part of the world, not branding pasted into the environment.
Use minimal pink crystal highlights and ancient stone architecture.
```

## Final Sanctum Prompt

```text
Create final sanctum concept art for Seismic Golem.

A massive central altar chamber where the network can awaken again. The chamber is the heart-memory of the sanctum: sacred, silent, architectural, and emotionally powerful. The golem approaches a central altar where pink crystal energy can reconnect the sleeping rails.

The scene should feel like:
- culmination
- ritual
- memory
- reactivation

Avoid cliché boss arena design. This is not about spectacle through clutter, but gravity through composition.
```
